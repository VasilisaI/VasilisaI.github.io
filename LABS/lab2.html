System Set Up

AD0_VAL represents the last bit of the I2C address used by the IMU. On our system, this bit is set to 1 to match the physical address jumper configuration.

Signal Filtering

Analysis revealed noise peaks at 6Hz and 9Hz. I selected a 9Hz cutoff frequency. With a 30ms sampling rate ($T=0.03s$), the calculated $\alpha$ is 0.629.

Sensor Fusion: Complementary Filter

While the gyroscope is smooth, it suffers from drift. I implemented a Complementary Filter with $\alpha = 0.96$ to trust the gyro for fast motion and the accelerometer for long-term stability.

Stunt & Observations
